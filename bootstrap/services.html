For a memorable meal the quality of the service is something that guests often remember as much as the food and drink served.

TABLE SERVICE:

First and foremost, a restaurant service model is the method used to prepare, present, and distribute foods to consumers. By choosing the most profitable service model, restaurants can create an efficient workflow to streamline food delivery and table turnover.

COUNTER SERVICE

Each guest is assigned an order number that is called by the kitchen staff once it is ready. After retrieving their meals, customers can seat themselves or take their orders to-go.
